About me

Hi, my name is Erika Bejan and I am a passionate photographer. Ever since I can remember, I have found photography to be a form of expression and a way to capture life's most beautiful moments. I love exploring different techniques and perspectives to create images that evoke emotions and tell stories. When I'm not behind the camera, you can often find me outdoors, chasing the perfect light or searching for unique subjects to photograph. Photography has become my way of interpreting the world and appreciating its beauty in all its forms. Through my work, I aim to capture the essence of the people, places, and things that inspire me.
